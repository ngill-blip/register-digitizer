From paper register to unified patient record

A working proof of concept: photograph a handwritten TB lab register, and an AI vision model reads every row into structured, reviewable data — then links it across the disconnected systems (microscopy, GeneXpert, chest X-ray/CAD, spirometry).
Proof of concept
What's real vs. illustrative. The register photo below is a genuine field register from a Level 3 TB lab (patient names redacted, phone numbers masked & image blurred for the public version by the team). Every value in the extracted table was read by an AI vision model directly from this handwritten page — not typed by hand. The cross-system links in the final step (chest X-ray/CAD, spirometry) are illustrative placeholders to show the end goal; in production each system's register is scanned the same way. This file runs entirely offline — no data leaves your device.
